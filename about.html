About Me

As a skilled Network Engineer with three years of hands-on experience, I specialize in network security, design, managed services, and troubleshooting. My expertise includes optimizing network performance and implementing robust security measures. I have successfully designed, implemented, and managed network infrastructures, including LAN/WAN, routers, switches, firewalls and cloud technologies. I am following a BEng (Hons) in Computer Networking and Cloud Security from London Metropolitan University and had National Diploma in Technology in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering Technology from the University of Moratuwa (NDT-ITUM). Additionally, I have earned multiple certifications for Network Security, Network and System Administration, Cloud, Linux, and DevOps.

I am deeply interested in new technologies and continuously update my knowledge in areas such as Networking, Cloud, IoT, Linux and Windows, Cyber Security, and DevOps. I also have skills and knowledge in electrical engineering, PLC programming, and OT security. I am committed to staying at the forefront of technological advancements and applying this knowledge to drive innovation and efficiency in my work.
